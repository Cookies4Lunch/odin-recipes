BBQ Chicken Sandwich

Description

BBQ chicken sandwiches are an easy and hearty meal, perfect for lunch and dinner.

The sandwiches require very few ingredients and little prep time.

    Ingredients

  • 1 Bolillo Roll
  • 2 fully cooked chicken tenders
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Mayonaise

    Steps

  1. Heat a skillet on medium-high heat
  2. Add chicken and BBQ Sauce to the skillet
  3. Stir until the sauce begins to bubble
  4. Cut the bolillo bun in half and toast it
  5. Once the bolillo is toasted, spread mayonaise on both halves
  6. Add the chicken in sauce to the bolillo
  7. Cut in half and enjoy!
