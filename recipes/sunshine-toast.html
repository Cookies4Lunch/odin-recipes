Sunshine Toast

Description

Sunshine toast is a simple and quick breakfast that is easy to learn

You can add cheese or season to taste with your choice of spices and seasonings

Ingredients

  • 1 Slice of Bread
  • 1 Egg
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet on medium heat
  2. Use a small cup or dish to cut a round hole in the center of the bread
  3. Add butter to the pan
  4. Place the slice of bread and the small recently cut round into the pan
  5. Break the egg into the center portion of the slice of bread
  6. Cook until the bottom of the egg turns solid
  7. Flip both the slice of bread and the small round of bread over
  8. Periodically check the underside of the toast to see if the egg is cooked
  9. Remove from the pan
  10. Plate and enjoy!
