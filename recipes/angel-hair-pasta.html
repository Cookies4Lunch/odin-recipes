Angel Hair Pasta

Description

Angle hair pasta is a simple recipe that can be made in under 30 min.

This pasta is great because it can be customized with additional protein such as chicken or shrimp.

Ingredients

  • Capellini Pasta
  • Butter
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. Cook pasta according to the driections on the package
  2. Once pasta is at desired consistencey, strain and return to pot
  3. Add 1 1/2 tbsp of butter to the pot
  4. Add 2 handfuls of Parmesean cheese to the pot
  5. Finely chop the parsley and add to the pot
  6. Enjoy!
